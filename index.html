Various Services That We Offered

We Have All Your Dental Needs Covered & offered more than 14+ treatments

1.

Surgery of impacted 3rd Molar

We do Surgery for the last teeth which is hidden under gingiva or bellow occlusal level.

2.

Prosthesis of missing teeth

Complete denture for all missing teeth and removable partial denture for some missing teeth.

3.

Root Canal Treatment

Removing the pulp of teeth and filling it with Gutta purcha. It’s a method for saving cariously exposed tooth.

4.

Orthodontic Healing

For Spacing in teeth, wrong bite, teeth overlaps with each other, Severe inclined or proclined teeth and various cases we do Ortho Treatment.

5.

Smile Designing

If you have space in teeth and rotated teeth or broken teeth which affect your smile we can bring back your health smile again.

6.

Extractions

Painless Extraction for anterior teeth starts from Rs 300 only & for 3rd molar impaction 1k only.

7.

Teeth Whitening​

Depending on case Vinner or Bleaching should be done for a white glowing smile.

8.

Braces & Implants​

For Missing teeth Implant is done to drill in the bone & fix teeth in the bony socket. It’s a long term treatment.
